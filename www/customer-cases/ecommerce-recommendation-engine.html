Customer case: E-commerce Recommendation Engine Provider

Successful expansion to 2 new markets • Improved sales rep performance by 28%

About the customer

Our customer is a few years old e-commerce recommendation engine vendor. They started out as a small startup helping a handful of mid-level e-commerce businesses with on-site personalised product recommendations. In the past year they’ve hit a few big milestones, proved their product value and business case. Subsequently they raised a substantial VC led A round.

Customer problem

After raising a big round of funding, our customer was presented with an expectation of quick new market expansion. Since they haven’t done foreign market expansion before, they faced a few unique challenges:

  • how to approach a new, unknown, market,
  • how to estimate the potential market size,
  • where to get the contact and contextual information about the companies in that market.

On top of that, the customer started significantly ramping up the sales team, which presented them with a few additional challenges:

  • how to generate a constant flow of quality leads to “feed” the bigger sales pipeline capacity,
  • hot to equip new, unexperienced sales reps with a lot of contextual information about their prospects (so they don’t spend a lot of time doing sales research),
  • how to estimate the number of needed new sales reps to match the new revenue milestones.

Pipetop solution

Pipetop’s approach is simple: since we maintain the biggest, constantly updated database of e-commerce businesses with a lot of extra contextual information about them, we are able map complete local markets. Then we can further segment them to suit the needs of our different customers.

This is how we solved the business problems for our customer:

  • we looked at the existing won and lost customer cases (from the initial market),
  • in collaboration with our customer, we determined the most suitable market segment,
  • we estimated the size of adjacent local markets and thus helped our customer hire the right amount of sales reps,
  • we started delivering fresh new leads for this market to their CRM as soon as the new hires were ready to start selling,
  • we equipped the sales team with enough contextual information that enabled our customer to get new sales reps up to speed quickly and start delivering above expected conversion results within 2 months.

Hard facts

  • helped the client to successfully scale the sales team to 2 new markets (so far),
  • improved the number of contacted leads/day by 13%,
  • improved contact-to-demo conversion rates from initial market to new markets by 34%,
  • improved the number of sold deals for an average sales person/month by 28%.
